Website Evaluations

Youtube

Youtube is a great website that was created so people can bring entertainment to others. Initially it had a small start but is now ver succesfull.

  • Contrast - There is a difference between dark mode and light mode the first thing that you will see with light mode is there is white background with balck text this combined with the youtube videos that pop up make a very nice contrast. If you go dark mode however the contrast is the complete opposite, overall very nice on the eyes.
  • Repetition - The font and colors across the whole webpage are very consistent and are repeatadly seen..
  • Alignment - The allignment is very well sorted out with a navigation bar for quick access on the left side of the page and everything else in the right part along with the text under each video being a little bit indented leaving space for the profile picture.
  • Proximity - No matter what page you might go on you will always have the handy navigation bar on the left side of the page therefore making it really easy on the user to navigate through everything.
